WonderBear is an **AI Storybook Projector** made for children aged 3–8. Your child speaks, AI turns their words into a one-of-a-kind illustrated storybook, projected across the whole wall — not just storytime, but story-making time.

## 三、核心功能 / Key Features

### 1. 看图识字 — 独家免费 / Picture-Word Literacy — Exclusive & Free

**独家免费**的看图识字功能，根据权威学龄前儿童识字要求定制，大屏识字比手机/平板更友好、更护眼。支持 **15 种语言**的在线课程及练习。

Our signature **free** picture-word literacy program, built to authoritative preschool reading standards. Big-screen learning is friendlier and gentler on the eyes than phones or tablets. Available as online courses and practice in **15 languages**.

- **母语教学法 / Native teaching methods**：每种语言按其真实母语拼读体系分级（英语 Phonics、法语 Borel-Maisonny、德语 Fibel、日语五十音、韩语谚文、泰语辅音元音、阿拉伯字母…），不是机械翻译。
  *Each language is staged by its real native phonics system (English Phonics, French Borel-Maisonny, German Fibel, Japanese 50-on, Korean Hangul, Thai, Arabic…), not machine translation.*
- **图—词—句—音四位一体 / Image · Word · Sentence · Sound**：每个字配精美水彩插图、例句与发音，看图认字、自然记忆。
  *Every word pairs a watercolor illustration, an example sentence and pronunciation — recognition through pictures, memory through context.*
- **闭环学习 / A learning loop**：孩子用刚学会的字去共创新故事——学完即用，识字与创作打通。
  *Children reuse the words they just learned to co-create new stories — learn it, then use it.*

> 识字课程语种（15）/ Literacy languages (15): 中文 · English · Español · Français · Deutsch · Italiano · Português · 日本語 · 한국어 · ไทย · Tiếng Việt · العربية · Polski · Română · Русский
>
> 语音/UI 覆盖更广：语音 75 种（33 已接）、UI 33 种。完整对照见 [`language-support.md`](language-support.md)。
> *Voice/UI reach further: 75 voice langs (33 active), 33 UI langs. Full table: [`language-support.md`](language-support.md).*

### 2. 故事馆 / Story Library

不断更新的精美绘本，绘声绘色。绘本会对孩子要识的字做**特别标注**，方便边读边记。

A growing library of beautifully illustrated, narrated storybooks. Target literacy words are **specially highlighted** in each book, so reading and memorizing happen together.

### 3. AI 语音共创 — 注册后永久免费 / AI Voice Co-creation — Free Forever

为儿童精心设计的**讲故事智能体**，帮助孩子共创故事。孩子说话，AI 引导、提问、把想法编织成完整故事框架。**AI 语音功能永久免费。**

A storytelling AI agent designed for children that helps them co-create stories. The child talks; the AI guides, asks, and weaves their ideas into a complete story. **The AI voice feature is free forever.**

- **多语言 / Multilingual**：AI 语音讲故事支持 **75 种语言**（33 种已接入，其余加一行声音映射即可启用）。
  *AI voice storytelling supports **75 languages** (33 active; the rest are one mapping line away).*
- **语言怎么定 / How language is set**：用户在 **H5 注册页选择语言** → 设备 AI 语音即用该语言讲故事；在 **TV 界面也可随时手动切换**语言，**最终以 TV UI 当前语言为准**。
  *Users pick their language on the **H5 registration page** → the device tells stories in that language. They can also **switch anytime on the TV**, and the **current TV UI language always takes final precedence**.*

### 4. 免费赠送一本绘本 / One Free Storybook

注册即免费赠送**一本在线生成的精美绘本**权益——零成本体验完整共创流程。

Register and get **one free AI-generated storybook** — experience the full co-creation journey at no cost.
